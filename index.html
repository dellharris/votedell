GO Team

What is the GO Team?

Governance Body

The GO Team is the official school governance council for every Atlanta Public Schools campus — a collaborative body that shapes school direction alongside the principal.

Who's On It

The team includes parents, teachers, community members, and school staff — all elected by their peers to represent the Hope-Hill community.

What It Decides

The GO Team reviews and approves the School Improvement Plan, budget priorities, and school-level policies that affect students and families directly.

How It Meets

Meetings are open to the public and held monthly. Parents and community members are always welcome to attend, observe, and weigh in during public comment.
