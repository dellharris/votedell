About Dell

A parent who shows up — and gets things done.

You probably know me as Jude Couch's dad. I'm Dell Harris — a proud Hope-Hill parent running for the GO Team because I believe our kids deserve more than the basics. They deserve real resources, real opportunities, and adults in their corner who know how to make things happen.

I've spent my career building partnerships, solving problems, and creating programs that didn't exist before. I want to bring that same energy to Hope-Hill — not as a politician, but as a parent who's all in for this community.

  • Former Technology Chair, Grant Park Elementary

Voting

Your vote takes less than 2 minutes.

1

Check your email

Look for a message from ElectionBuddy. Check your Spam, Promotions, or Junk folders if you don't see it in your inbox.

2

Or check your texts

If your cell number is in Infinite Campus, you may have received a link via text from APS GO Team.

3

One vote per household

Only one guardian per household receives the link. Check with others in your home to see who got it.

4

Can't find your link?

Request your ballot at apsstrongschools.com or call APS at 404-802-2885.
